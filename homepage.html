West End Civic Association


Previous Next

The West End Civic Association is the oldest civic association in the Village of Floral Park founded in 1920. It services residents from the east side of Daisy Avenue all the way to Larch Avenue.

Monthly meetings are held the first Thursday of every month beginning October through June (excluding December). Members convene at 8:00 pm at the Atlantic Avenue Firehouse, second floor. All citizens are welcome.

Please note that construction is slated to begin on The Atlantic Avenue Firehouse. The work is slated to begin in June and last until late October. We have moved the venue of our next meeting. Please join us on Thursday June 7th at 8:00PM in the Daisy Room at the Floral Park Recreation Center.
